Fantastic bathroom renovation by De BadkamerVakman, highly recommended. After searching for a reliable and expert company to renovate our bathroom for two years, we finally found De BadkamerVakman in Wateringen. From the very first contact, we had a good feeling about them. Not only because of the pleasant communication, but also because of the excellent price-quality ratio. What was especially important to us: the bathroom was in a historic building in Leiden, where nothing was straight – the walls, floors, and ceilings were all crooked. This presented a considerable challenge, especially considering the spacious dimensions of the bathroom, approximately 2.5 by 5 meters. The result? We are more than satisfied – it turned out beautifully. From the very beginning, the renovation process was very professional. The project began with a well-thought-out design process. We already had a rough idea of ​​the new layout, which saved us a lot of time. In addition, we were incredibly supported by the tips and practical advice we received during this phase. The two visits to the showroom in Wateringen and the initial consultation at our home were valuable moments during which our wishes were perfectly translated into a concrete design. The time and effort invested in this preparation paid off handsomely. The renovation itself took four weeks due to the historic building. In the first two days, the old bathroom was completely demolished and neatly removed using a skip provided by the company. Then the true craftsman got to work. And what craftsmanship he demonstrated! In a crooked space, he installed the wall tiles with the utmost precision. Everything looks symmetrical and finished down to the smallest detail. Moreover, he worked remarkably neatly: no mess, no disruption, just expert work. As discerning clients – especially considering previous renovations in our historic building – we can honestly say that BadkamerVakman is among our absolute top renovation companies. We have rarely encountered a company that works so professionally, thoughtfully, and meticulously. We therefore wholeheartedly recommend BadkamerVakman to anyone looking for quality, reliability and a beautiful end result.
